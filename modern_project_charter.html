∞ Opportunities Saved

This project will create an interactive solution that helps small businesses market themselves more effectively to local clients. By integrating AI technologies such as phone-based digital assistants and automated responses for chat and email, the project will provide businesses with smarter, faster ways to interact with customers.

These tools will not only enhance customer engagement but also reduce missed opportunities by ensuring that inquiries are answered quickly and professionally. The outcome will support small business owners in growing their visibility, attracting new clients, and building lasting customer relationships in their local communities.

Current Semester

In Scope for Semester Project:

  • Creating multiple avenues for AI responses: phone systems, chat, and emails.
  • Creating a webpage that will incorporate this into a singular solution.
  • Developing simple workflows so businesses can manage these AI responses in one place.
  • Testing the solution with real small business scenarios to ensure it improves client interaction and local visibility.
6 Month Roadmap

Roadmap Items within 6 Months:

  • Building a website that will automatically allow businesses and clients to create their own referral network.
  • AI will then recommend different businesses based on their location, services offered, as well as showing ranks based on Google reviews.
  • Adding collaboration features so businesses can cross promote each other within their referral networks.
  • Expanding recommendations to include additional credibility indicators such as response speed or customer satisfaction scores.

Out of Scope:

  • Creating any type of app.
  • Offering 24/7 support solutions.
  • Moving this to a national scale. This project is intended to be local and even potentially hyper local to cities and possibly counties.
